Complete OPTP Menu And Prices – Pakistan

OPTP (One Potato Two Potato),is a successful fast food brand of Pakistan. Claiming that our fries are the best and the best in quality. Their standout offering, Hand Cut Fries, has garnered a dedicated following.

These uniquely crafted fries come in a variety of delectable options. The Plain Fries, made from the freshest potatoes, promise a mesmerizing taste. For an even more enjoyable experience, consider pairing them with a refreshing drink. Additionally, the BBQ Fries are highly recommended for those seeking an alternative option.
